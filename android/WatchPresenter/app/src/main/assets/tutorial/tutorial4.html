Control with your phone

That's it! Now you can control the presentation with your phone

Simply open the WatchPresenter app on your phone and use the volume buttons to control it.

  • Volume up: next slide
  • Volume down: previous slide

You can even switch the screen off if you want to save battery and avoid distractions.

Note: Using the volume buttons while using WatchPresenter does not modify the riger volume, so you don't have to worry about that!
